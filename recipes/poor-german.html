The poor German

Description

That is an easy dish that has been made by Swiss people who are not rich. It's a simple potato dish.

Duration: 30min

Ingredients

The following declarations are measured for 2 portions

  • 4 potatoes
  • 1 onion
  • 1 garlic
  • 1dl Knorr stock
  • 200g minced bacon (If you can afford that)

Steps

  1. Peel and wash the potatoes.
  2. Slice them into ca. 2 cm wide cubes. Boil them for about 1o minutes.
  3. Meanwhile you can dice the garlic and slice the onion.
  4. Put oil in a pan over high heat and sear the garlic and onion. After about 5-6 minutes the potatoes should be ready to take out of the water.
  5. You may add bacon to the pan now.
  6. Take the potatoes out of the water, dry them of and add them to your frying pan. You might want to add some more oil and fry everything together for a couple of minutes until the potatoes turn golden brown.
  7. Add your stock.
  8. Your dish is ready to plate and then serve.
